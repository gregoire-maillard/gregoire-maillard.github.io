About this page

I have been learning Turkish since November 2022.
On this page which I will complete over time, I intend to gather ressources that I found the most useful for the process of learning Turkish, as well as different cultural ressources to make learning more fun!

Before all

Here are different ressources I personally use and enjoy while learning turkish

  • Duolingo:
    It can be a great introduction to the language and a simple way to keep studying on a daily basis! It lacks of some fundamentals explanations but will end up teaching you +1k words. You can add me: here.

  • Duome:
    It's a great ressource full of tips and notes on what is missing on Duolingo. Overall, a great complement to any study. You can find it here: here.
  • Grammar books:
    My favorite and most useful book was 45 leçons pour parler turc which explains very clearly many concepts and makes the correct parallel with french language. Many exercises guide the learning. You can buy it: here.
